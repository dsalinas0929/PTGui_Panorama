Summary
I am looking for an experienced panorama/PTGui specialist to help me process a large archive of 360° timelapse footage captured with Tikee Pro 3 cameras (dual fisheye, Enlaps).
The project consists of two parts:

Part 1 – Template Development

I have footage from 2–3 fixed camera positions covering a long-term construction documentation project. For each position, I need a calibrated PTGui template that delivers:

Correct fisheye lens mapping for the Tikee Pro 3 dual-lens system
Accurate horizon levelling and straight-line geometry
Proper distortion correction and exposure blending
Clean equirectangular output suitable for 360° viewing and timelapse assembly

I will provide sample image pairs from multiple perspectives and different times of day (varying light conditions) to support calibration. The goal is a robust template that holds up across the full dataset – not just a single showcase stitch.

Part 2 – Batch Processing Pipeline

Once templates are validated, I need a reliable automated workflow to process approximately 5,000–20,000 dual-fisheye image pairs through PTGui in batch mode, producing equirectangular panoramas ready for timelapse use.
The pipeline should include:

Scripted batch execution (PTGui CLI or equivalent automation)
Error logging – identifying failed or incomplete stitches
Consistent output naming and folder structure
Ideally: parallel processing to make efficient use of available hardware

Optional – Timelapse Assembly

If you have experience assembling equirectangular image sequences into 360° timelapse videos (FFmpeg, DaVinci Resolve, or similar), feel free to include this as an optional add-on in your proposal.

What I will provide:

Sample RAW/JPEG image pairs from all 3 camera positions
Context on desired output resolution and format
Feedback during template validation

What I expect from you:

Demonstrable experience with PTGui Pro and fisheye/360° workflows
Familiarity with dual-lens action or timelapse cameras (Insta360, Tikee, Ricoh, or similar is a plus)
Clear communication and milestone-based delivery

Please include examples of previous panorama or batch processing work in your proposal.
